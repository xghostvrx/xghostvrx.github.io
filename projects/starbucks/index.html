What's New

  • Spring Beverage Lineup

    Indulge in our new lineup of refreshing spring beverages, including the Floral Macchiato and the Honey Lavender Latte. These floral-infused drinks are the perfect way to welcome the season.

  • Plant-Based Options

    We're excited to introduce more plant-based options to our menu, including the Beyond Meat Breakfast Sandwich and the Vegan Chocolate Chip Cookie. Enjoy delicious, sustainable choices that are good for you and the planet.

  • Reusable Cup Program

    Join our efforts to reduce waste by participating in our reusable cup program. Purchase a reusable cup and receive a discount on your drink every time you use it.

  • Digital Rewards Program

    Sign up for our digital rewards program and earn points with every purchase. Redeem points for free drinks, food, and more.

  • New Merchandise

    Check out our new merchandise, including limited-edition mugs, tumblers, and coffee accessories. Add a touch of Starbucks style to your home or office.

  • Community Events

    Join us for community events, such as coffee tastings, live music, and art exhibitions. Connect with other coffee lovers and enjoy a unique café experience.

  • Seasonal Treats

    Indulge in our seasonal treats, such as the Easter-themed Egg Cake Pop and the Spring-themed Sugar Cookies. Perfect for celebrating the season with a sweet treat.

  • Expanded Breakfast Menu

    Start your day off right with our expanded breakfast menu, featuring new items like the Avocado Toast and the Acai Bowl. Fuel your day with delicious and nutritious options.

  • New Coffee Blends

    Discover our new coffee blends, carefully crafted to deliver rich flavor and aroma. Try the Ethiopia Single-Origin or the Guatemala Antigua for a truly exceptional coffee experience.

  • Mobile Ordering

    Skip the line and order ahead with our mobile ordering app. Enjoy the convenience of ordering and paying from your phone, then pick up your order at your convenience.
